# 💼 Consultancy & Strategic Impact Portfolio
### *Lead Investigator & Qualitative Specialist*

This portfolio highlights key independent and contracted consultancies involving high-stakes evidence synthesis, diversity governance, and clinical implementation evaluation.

---

## 🎗️ Cancer Council Victoria (CCV)
**Role:** Lead Investigator
**Project:** Diversity Plan Implementation Evaluation

* **Objective:** To evaluate the organizational implementation and impact of the CCV Diversity Plan across clinical and community sectors.
* **Methodology:** Applied **Implementation Science** frameworks to assess organizational change, community engagement, and the efficacy of diversity protocols.
* **Outcome:** Provided critical data to drive inclusive policy development and identified key barriers to equitable cancer care delivery.

---

## 🏥 Western Cancer Memorial Integrated Cancer Service (WCMICS)
**Role:** Lead Investigator
**Project:** Overview of Systematic Reviews of Communication in Cancer Care

* **Objective:** To synthesize a massive volume of evidence regarding clinician-patient communication models in oncology.
* **Methodology:** Conducted an "Overview of Reviews" (Umbrella Review), evaluating the quality and clinical utility of existing systematic review data.
* **Outcome:** Developed a high-level evidence base used to inform communication training and patient-centered care strategies for cancer clinicians.

---

## 🔬 Cancer Australia & Cancer Council Victoria
**Role:** Qualitative Research Specialist
**Project:** Cancer Diagnosis in Culturally Diverse Populations

* **Objective:** To investigate the factors influencing cancer diagnosis pathways and cultural beliefs among diverse Australian communities.
* **Methodology:** Utilized advanced qualitative methodologies and **Thematic Analysis** (Dedoose) to explore diagnostic delay, health literacy, and cultural perceptions of cancer.
* **Impact:** This research directly informed the development of culturally sensitive diagnostic tools and public health messaging.

---

## 🏥 Western Health & WCMICS
**Role:** Contracted Specialist
**Focus:** Clinical Governance & Evidence Implementation

* **Overview:** Provided expert consultancy on integrating academic research findings into frontline clinical environments.
* **Focus Areas:** Bridging the gap between systematic evidence and hospital-based clinical workflows, focusing on patient safety and implementation fidelity.

---

## 🏗️ Methodological Specialization
In each of these consultancies, I applied a "Senior Project Manager" lens to ensure academic rigor met real-world operational needs:
* **Fidelity Auditing:** Ensuring programs were implemented as designed.
* **Acceptability Assessment:** Evaluating if clinical tools and diversity plans were embraced by the workforce.
* **Translation:** Converting dense academic data into strategic "Executive Reports" for government and NGO stakeholders.
